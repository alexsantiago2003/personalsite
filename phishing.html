Phishing

Phishing attacks are where you are emailed, called, or texted false information in order to get something from you. There could be a link that directs you to a malicious site or downloads malicious software onto your computer. There could also be a caller that claims to need personal information. These can be avoided by not clicking suspicious links and blocking numbers that you don’t know.
